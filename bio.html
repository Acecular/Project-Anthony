    Hi! I am an undergraduate at the University of California, Berkeley. As a member of the Berkeley community, I am involved with the Associated Students of the University of California (ASUC), the largest and most autonomous student government in the country. One of the highlights of my week as a student is also being able to mentor under-served children in Oakland as part of the EBAYC, Oakland Asian Student Educational Services (OASES).

    Born and raised in the Bay Area, I have a deep commitment to the people of the local community. I graduated from Mission San Jose High School where I swam varsity for four years and was a year-round club swimmer for many years. Now, after many years, I continue to be around the pool but on the pool deck where I continue my love of swimming: coaching younger swimmers.

    If I can help in any way, please do not hesitate to email me.
