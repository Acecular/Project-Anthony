    Fall 2016

    This semester was my first exposure in internal accountability within the ASUC and all that it does for our student body. As the Director of Accountability, my goals were to primarily enforce attendance at committee and Senate meetings. I also made sure weekly and bi-weekly reports were submitted for executive officials and senators that accurately reflected what our officials were doing for the student body/campus. I also sat in on a couple Governance and Internal Affairs and Senate Meetings to discuss Senate Resolutions, interviewed potential nominees for Judicial Council and gained experience with the inner workings of our student government. At the end of the semester, I also helped Chief Accountability Officer Kenny Chen and Chief of Staff Isabel Chen gather and arrange the stipends for the 40+ association officials. The mid-year report was also due to our office at the end of the semester.
